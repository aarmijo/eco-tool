ProSEco Eco-tool

Use the links above to edit a process, build a product system and perform LCA calculations upon the selected system.

A process can represent a single life cycle stage of a product, the whole life cycle of a product or the sub processes and sub products that constitute the life cycle stages.

Once the processes are created to model a system and tied together, a product system can be built and the environmental impact calculated.
